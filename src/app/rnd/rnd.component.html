PowderRange

PowderRange is a comprehensive range of premium quality off-theshelf powders and bespoke materials made to your specification based on pure metals, alloys, machine type, application and research needs. We employ a range of techniques including Inert Gas Atomisation, Vacuum Inert Gas Atomisation, Plasma Atomisation and Plasma Spheroidisation to develop powders for LMD (Laser Metal Deposition), SLM (Selective Laser Melting) and EBM (Electron Beam Melting).

Different AM systems require different powder characteristics to produce high-quality parts, LPW powders are designed and produced to perform reliably and consistently in your AM machine for your particular application.

  • All powder is supplied with a Certificate of Conformance that contains test data for chemistry and sizing.
  • All chemical data is performed at an independent ISO 17025 / Nadcap certified laboratory.
  • In addition to the standard Certificates of Conformance, we can offer enhanced Powder Evaluation Reports, which include Hall Flow, Angle of Repose, Tap Density, Apparent Density, Morphology, Pycnometry, Shape Analysis, Moisture, Rheometry, and Contamination Analysis.
  • All powders are packaged in tamper evident HDPE containers chosen for robustness and resistance to moisture ingress.
  • SLM and EBM powders are packed with non-contaminating silica gel sachets.
  • Titanium alloys are packed under a high purity Argon atmosphere; non-reactive alloys are packed under normal air atmosphere. If requested, we can pack any material under Argon.

We can supply small volumes, facilitate R&D projects, prototyping and specialist niche products and offer long term supply agreements for larger volumes.

Custom Powders

For many alloys LPW is able to offer different products of the same composition, optimised for a particular application.

For example the use of different atomisation gases to vary mechanical properties, enhanced flowability of powder for ‘flow-critical’ machine platforms, lower residual elements for more demanding applications, controlled interstitials for enhanced mechanical properties. If the metal powder you’re looking for is not on our list of available materials, or you have a new material in mind, we have an experienced application team and the flexibility to:

  • Develop new powder chemistries to deliver specific properties
  • Explore novel alloys
  • Investigate compositions for new applications

Plasma Spheroidised Powders

At LPW we have a high level of expertise in Additive Manufacturing (AM) and a long experience of working with leading companies within the aerospace, biomedical, and automotive industries. We utilise this knowledge and the capabilities of plasma technology to produce highly spherical and low contamination powders:

  • High melting temperature refractory metals such as Ta, W, Nb, and Mo
  • Customer specific metallic and ceramic compositions
  • Improve flowability and reduce contamination levels of standard products produced by gas or water atomisation
  • Reconditioning of metallic powders that have been used several times within an AM machine. Powder purity, morphology, and surface contamination change during use, especially Oxygen, Nitrogen, and Hydrogen
